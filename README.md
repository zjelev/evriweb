# evriweb
A CRM and producion orders system developed in JSF and MySQL for my Masters Degree way back in 2008

Functionalities:
1. Register incoming requests from potential clients.
2. Register outgoing messages to clients and suppliers.
3. Tracking employees' portfolio of clients and suppliers.
4. Assigning and watching tasks to employees.
5. Putting internal orders for production from traders to the factory.
6. Supply chain and sales management.
7. All of the above in accordance with ISO 9001:2000 quality standard implemented in the company.
